## 12-Week Raffle Marketing Calendar Template

A drop-in marketing calendar for a 12-week 50/50 cycle. Designed to be the starting template you customize with your brand voice, not a rigid playbook.

Every row assumes:
- **Day 0 = cycle launch.**
- **Day 84 = draw day.**
- Four core channels (email, SMS, paid social, PR/local media).
- A fifth optional channel (radio / billboard) for programs with the budget.

---

### Pre-launch (Days -14 to -1)

**Email**
- Day -14: Save-the-date to full list. Soft tease of prize and cause.
- Day -7: "Launch next week" email with countdown.
- Day -1: "Tomorrow" email, final mobilization.

**SMS** — none until launch (save opt-ins for live phase).

**Paid social** — warm-up creative to build pixel audience. Low spend.

**PR / local media** — press release embargoed until launch day. Beat reporters briefed.

---

### Week 1 — Launch week (Days 0–6)

**Email**
- Day 0 (launch): "It's live" email to full list at 9am local time.
- Day 3: First winner spotlight (secondary prize) or cause story.
- Day 6: "First week milestones" email.

**SMS**
- Day 0: Single SMS to opted-in supporters: "Tickets on sale now."

**Paid social**
- Launch-day creative across Meta, Instagram, Google.
- Geo-targeted to primary catchment first.
- Budget: roughly 15–20% of cycle's paid budget.

**PR / local media**
- Press release on launch morning.
- Interview with executive director on local AM radio.

**Operational reminder:** monitor checkout error rates; have support coverage above normal.

---

### Weeks 2–3 — Ramp (Days 7–20)

**Email**
- Weekly: Monday (jackpot update), Friday (cause story or beneficiary spotlight).

**SMS** — none. Save frequency budget.

**Paid social**
- Sustain spend. Test creative variants.
- Expand audience geography as jackpot grows.

**PR / local media**
- First milestone press release ($250K or $500K, whichever hits first).
- Pitch a feature story to local daily paper.

---

### Weeks 4–8 — Build (Days 21–55)

**Email**
- Weekly Monday + Friday cadence continues.
- Add a mid-week "behind the scenes" email every 2 weeks.

**SMS**
- Single mid-cycle SMS at Day 35 for a meaningful milestone.

**Paid social**
- Increase spend 20–30% in this window. This is the compounding period.
- Introduce video creative featuring past winners or beneficiaries.

**PR / local media**
- $1M milestone press release.
- Reach out to regional TV news for a segment.
- Secure local radio spots if budget permits.

---

### Weeks 9–10 — Mid-tempo push (Days 56–69)

**Email**
- Weekly cadence + "halfway home" motivational email.
- Add a testimonial email featuring past supporters.

**SMS** — none.

**Paid social**
- Sustained high spend.
- Consider retargeting campaigns to email openers who haven't purchased.

**PR / local media**
- Community partnership announcements.

---

### Week 11 — Penultimate (Days 70–76)

**Email**
- Monday: "One week to go" email.
- Wednesday: Cause impact email (stories of what proceeds will fund).
- Friday: "Final weekend to secure tickets" email.

**SMS**
- Single SMS: "One week until draw. Tickets live at [URL]."

**Paid social**
- Escalate spend 50% above prior week.
- Creative shifts to urgency: "Final week" messaging.

**PR / local media**
- Final milestone press release.
- Pitch draw-day preview to local outlets.

---

### Week 12 — Final week (Days 77–84)

**Email**
- Daily emails for the final 3 days.
- Morning and evening sends on draw day.

**SMS**
- Day 81: "Final 72 hours" SMS.
- Day 83: "Last day" SMS.
- Day 84: "Draw at 7pm tonight" SMS.

**Paid social**
- Maximum spend. This is where 30–40% of cycle revenue can be earned.
- Near-continuous creative rotation.
- Story ads with countdown timers.

**PR / local media**
- Draw-day press release.
- Live coverage of the draw if possible (community station, Facebook Live).
- Winner announcement release within 24 hours of the draw.

---

### Post-cycle (Days 85–100)

**Email**
- Day 85: Winner announcement to full list.
- Day 90: Thank-you email with aggregate impact.
- Day 100: "Here's what your tickets funded" email with specific beneficiaries.

**Paid social** — wind down.

**PR / local media** — winner story coverage.

**Critical operational task:** full post-cycle retrospective within 14 days of cycle close.

---
